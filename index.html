Our Story

Founded in Paris in 1985, Lumière brings authentic French baking techniques to every creation. Our head pastry chef, Claire Dubois, trained under Michelin-starred chefs before bringing her artistry to our boutique bakery.

We source only the finest ingredients - French butter, organic flours, and seasonal fruits - to create pastries that are both visually stunning and unforgettable in taste.
